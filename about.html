About Water my Plants

Happy healthy plants, make for happy healthier people

Not everyone is a born gardener. Knowing when each plant needs to be watered doesn't come naturally. In comes our application. Having plants arount the house or office is healthy, keeping them alive is what we want to help you with

Adding the plants you have to your list allows you to add notifications and reminders to water them. We want to help take the guess work out of how often that Aloe plant needs water compared to your spider plant, or even those tomatoes you have outside on the porch
